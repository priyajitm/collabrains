Frequently Asked Questions

How to join a project?

After you complete the register, you start your process to meet, looking for projects regardless your skillset. We try to match the project with your skills, this way you work on the right project.

What is the pricing for work in a collaborative project?

There is no price to join CollaBrains.

Can I join to mentor others?

If you are a mentor or want to mentor anyone since you have the right skills, you came to the right place. You're welcome!

Why should I join to CollaBrains?

If you are looking for a real project experience, you came to the right place. On CollaBrains we want make sure to give the perfect environment for your learning path. Even if you never worked in team or in a real project, here is the right place to doi it.

I want to build a project I have in mind, can I join CollaBrains?

Absolutely! Everyone can bring their own ideas and share with the mentors the details about the project. After your idea was approved, we start working in team in your idea.

I came with an idea and we finish the project, this project is mine or the team?

Every project we build here, is from everyone. You can't say the project is only yours.

Can I share on my resumé/portfolio the projects I build here?

Absolutely! This way everyone has a real project on their resumé or portfolio.
